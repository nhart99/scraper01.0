## Notes on Portal Access

### No Login Required (Public Access)
- Most utility RFP/bid opportunity pages are publicly accessible
- Some require registration to download detailed RFP documents
- BC Hydro uses BC Bid (public viewing, no registration)

### Registration/Login Required for Full Access
- Hydro-Québec Supplier Space (for bid submission)
- OPG via Ariba Discovery
- Some utilities may require vendor registration for detailed documents

### Portal Types
- **Direct Utility Websites**: Most utilities
- **Third-Party Platforms**:
  - Wood Mackenzie/Ariba (Duke Energy, OPG)
  - BC Bid (BC Hydro)
  - Utility-specific supplier portals

## Adding More Utilities

To add additional utilities to the scraper, edit `config/utilities.json` and include:
- Unique ID
- Utility name
- Type (IOU, Municipal, Co-op, Gov)
- Region
- RFP URL
- Optional: procurement portal URL
- Scraper type (html, ariba, etc.)
- Active status (true/false)
